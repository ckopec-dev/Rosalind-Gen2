# Finding Disjoint Motifs in a Gene - Rosalind Problem Solution in Forth

## Problem Understanding

The problem asks us to find two disjoint motifs (substrings) in a DNA sequence such that:
1. The motifs are disjoint (don't overlap)
2. The motifs are of specified lengths
3. We need to find the positions where these motifs occur
